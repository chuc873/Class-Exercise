Do you have a something to say about how the climate crisis relates to you and your family?

How to contribute

This survey invites you to reflect or imagine about your personal relationships with climate change. Contributions from you will be aggregated and displayed on the Atolling Sovereignty platform as part of the MS Design and Urban Ecologies thesis project by Daniel Chu. See how I protect your privacy here.

To contribute, I invite you to think in one of the three themes:
1. How has the climate crisis affected you in the past, either your family history of your own upbringing?
2. Have you made major decisions about work, family, moving, or other important life events because of climate change?
3. What do you imagine a future where climate crisis does not affect your home look like? Are you hopeful for the future?
